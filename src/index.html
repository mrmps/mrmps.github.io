Team

We are a prototype development company specialized in area of atmospheric plasma. Dr.Yuri Glukhoy, the President, has 40 years experience in developing and commercializing scientific instruments in the area of plasma physics, ion and electron optics and has extensive industrial and scientific contacts with semiconductor companies like Intel, Micron and LAM research. He has designed and patented the plasma generating components for etching, deposition and implantation as well as consumables like large area showerheads for large area wafer processes. He has commercialized several innovations in the past 15 years relating to ICP systems and continues to develop and commercialize new products.
